Chicken

Description

This is Josie's favorite chicken recipe.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast (1.5lbs - 2lbs)
  • 1/2 Onion (Red or White)
  • 3 Garlic Cloves
  • Soy sauce
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Olive oil
  • (Optional) Chicken broth

Steps

  1. Cut onion and garlic
  2. Cut chicken into bite size chunks
  3. Heat olive oil in pan over medium heat
  4. Add garlic into heated pan
  5. Add onion into heated pan
  6. Let the garlic and onion cook for until golden
  7. Add the chicken
  8. Season chicken with salt and pepper
  9. Cook chicken until it reaches 165 degrees
  10. Turn heat to high and deglaze with soy sauce and (optional) chicken broth
  11. After pan is deglazed remove from heat and enjoy!
